About Kashmir

Welcome to "Discover Kashmir," your gateway to the enchanting beauty of the Kashmir Valley. Nestled in the northern part of the Indian subcontinent, Kashmir is a paradise on earth, renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality.

What to Explore:

Discover the timeless charm of Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, with its houseboats, Mughal gardens, and vibrant local markets. Explore the ancient city of Gulmarg, a haven for winter sports enthusiasts, offering world-class skiing and snowboarding opportunities. The quaint town of Pahalgam, surrounded by snow-capped mountains, provides a serene retreat for those seeking tranquility.
